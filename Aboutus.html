About

Anime Stocks is a small company based in the Philippines and was founded on May 19, 2021 by a team of three members. It was first was founded by Bermar Villarazo and Kelly Tom Cu in early April of 2021 and was later on co-founded by Yelroj Livve Bejoc in May of the same year.


The Founders and Co-Founder currently manage the Facebook page including its community group. The website was designed by Yelroj and developed along side with Kelly and Bermar and was published on June 18, 2022.


Aims and Mission

Anime Stocks aims to help the anime fans out there to look and discover any anime merchandise from manga to light novels. Our mission is to help the community by providing them with the best deals available while ensuring their safety and security so that they can purchase any products they want without the fear of encountering fraud and scams.
