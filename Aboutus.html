About us

Anime Stocks is a small company that helps the anime fans out there looking for anime merchandise, manga and light novels. Our mission is to help them by giving them the best deals as possible and also ensuring that they can purchase any products without a fear of getting scammed.


Anime Stocks was founded by Bermar Villarazo and Kelly Tom Cu in April 2020. Later on it was co-founded by Yelroj Livve Bejoc in May 2020. It was then introduced to the anime communities in Facebook. Yelroj Livve Bejoc designed the website and also the promoter of Anime Stocks. Villarazo and Cu developed the Animestocks website and published it in June 18 2022.
